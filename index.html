1. About Me

I have always been attracted to learning new things all my life and I love expending my knowledge in different areas.

Learning how to code has been a challenging but very rewarding journey for me. I am at a point where i can say that I am confident that I can build websites using my HTML, CSS, Bootstrap and Javascript skills. I have a solid understanding of Wordpress and GitHub version control as well.

I have spend a lot of time learning and practising my JavaScript skills and I am looking to expand my skill set by learning a JavaScript framework, including working with API's, functional programing and object orientated programing.

My goal is to grow as a developer and learn new technologies while staying up to date with the ones I know already. I would love to work with a motivated team that is passionate about their work and about what they create on a day to day basis as I believe this is the best way to move forward.

On a personal level, I am very proud of my family and I love spending time with thme with them. I also enjoy traveling and staying active.

2. My Experience

Throughout my career I had jobs in customer service, banking industry, property management and I am also running my own company in the photography industry.

After years of dreaming about working as a Web Developer, I finally took the decision to go for it. I have been making websites, web pages, small JavaScript applications and it's time to take it to the next level. I know that I found my real passion and I love every minute of it. I am so happy to learn new things every day and create stuff "on the internet" and I'm sure this will be a great journey for me.

I have a proven record of customer service skills in many industries. I had jobs in telecom companies, customer service jobs in the banking industry all the way up to a corporate relationship manager position in RBS Bank. Having my own company and dealing with customers is always challenging and rewarding.

Working for nearly 10 years in the banking industry gave me a very good ability of dealing with customers; listening and understanding their wishes and needs is very important for providing a great customer service.

Property management is a very dynamic role and requires great abilities of identifying a problem, finding the appropriate solution for it and making sure the problem gets fixed. I will refer to this skill as "debugging" from now on . Property management sometimes requires thinking outside the box in order to deal with day-to-day tasks, so it was a very good learning process for me.

Last but not least, my love for photography. A life-long passion turned into a business. I have been taking pictures for ever and, in the last 10 years, I have been able to make a business out of it. Photography is much more than a simple click of the camera shutter and I am very lucky to call this a job.
